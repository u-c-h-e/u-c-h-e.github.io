SOLUTION

A Supportive Platform for Human Traffic Survivors

Rise offers survivors a judgement-free, empathic support system and community. It empowers members to rebuild their lives through Rise's network and connects them to local resources to support their goals.

LESSONS LEARNED

Reflection

Lesson #1: Poor direction is the biggest mistake you could make

I would incorporate more user testing opportunities. User feedback from the high-fidelity mocks showed room to push features even further, such as provide more context to the toggle option for section for first-time users. It would have also been interesting to conduct Maze tests to see how easy or hard certain tasks are.
