ROOMIES, WE'VE GOT A PROBLEM

Shared adult households don't always have an efficient way of managing communal responsibilities

Living with roommates can be great—the decreased rent, built-in company, savings of furniture and appliance costs. But as most of us know, it can have its drawbacks. Have you ever walked into the kitchen and felt crumbs underneath your feet. Or, stepped into the bathroom only to see that the overflowing trash still hadn’t been taken out yet?

SOLUTION

A Collaborative, Communal Task Management App

HomeMakers is a collaborative app that makes managing communal tasks easy. With the app, housemates can see what others are doing to ensure that common areas are clean, know when it’s their turn to contribute to the house and avoid uncomfortable confrontations. Additional features include:

  1. Assigning tasks to specific housemates;
  2. Customizable task schedules;
  3. Notifications and reminders;
  4. History of tasks' completion over time

RESEARCH

Understanding User Needs & Frustrations

Findings from 117 survey participants and 8 guerrilla user interviews gave me insight into the current system housemates use for chore management, the type of frustrations and limitations targeted users currently faced, and dispel assumptions on what features potential users would find valuable.

FINAL PRODUCT

Refined Key Features After Testing

After re-evaluated some design decisions made previosly and testers' issues with the prototype, I went on the polish the app's aesticics and incorporate delight in the design.

Tasks ordered by due date

Users can access tasks they've been assigned (along with tasks open for anyone to do). From this screen housemates can:

  • Mark a task as completed
  • See the urgency / due date of takes
  • Sort tasks by area
  • Search for tasks

Productivity tracking

Users can access track their all household members' productivity regarding task completion. From this screen housemates can:

  • Track their contrbution over time
  • Track their housemates' contrbution
  • View streak for completing tasks
  • Gain motivation to continue contributing

Easy task set-up

Schedule with days, reminders, and a history for each task

  1. Assign a task to a housemates
  2. Set chore schedule
  3. Edit task with notes
  4. View the history of a task

ASSUMPTIONS

Setting eyes on the target

When it comes to files storage, most people fit into one of these groups:

  1. The Minimalist - uses Cloud storage to back-up essential files only.
  2. The Collector - uses Cloud storage for everything.
  3. The InBetweener - uses Cloud storage for a moderate amount of files.

My focus (and empathy) was with the In-Betweeners, as they generally have a wealth of content and sometimes find it difficult to keep track of everything. Knowing this, I wanted to create a versatile app that would help them organize their content in a streamlined and intuitive way.

LESSON #2

Surveys are helpful, but only when you have (proper) direction

Tied to lesson one, a symptom of having poor direction is not knowing the right questions to ask. If I could do this project over, I would have created a secondary survey to ask respondents about their specific behaviors and preferences when it comes to organizing content. Now I spend more time trying to understand problems users face before I begin working out solutions.
