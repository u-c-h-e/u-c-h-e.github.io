More About Me
A product sherpa once told me to it’s better to be an expert at one thing versus somewhat knowledgeable in many things. "A Jill of all trades is a master of none," he explained.

The complete original saying goes, “A jack of all trades is a master of none, but oftentimes better than a master of one.”

As a T-shaped designer, I sit at the happy medium between the two. I specialize in product design and problem solving and belong to the camp of people who can think laterally and apply insights from several fields.

My ability to apply design thinking in the product design process while focusing on end-to-end product development rather than just the design phase is one of many assets I bring to the table.
